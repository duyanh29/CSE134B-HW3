Wildlife

Seeing how fast our world has been changing positively in many aspects such as healthcare, medical, education, economy, trades, technology, etc. is so great, however, we are forgetting about our nature, the environment we're living and the wildlife where is home of millions of other species

As a result, our actions cause a great damage to the wildlife which leads to many problems. Below are some of the greatest threats that wildlife is facing, according to NWF:

  • Global warming
  • Climate change
  • Habitat loss
  • Disease
  • Pollution
  • Invasive species
  • Overexploitation

Because of our carelessness, many species has become extinct. And if we don't take action now, we may not be able to save our nature, to save the other species:

  • Splendid Poison Frog: Estimated Extinction Date: 2020



  • Spix's Macaw



  • Northern White Rhinoceros: Estimated Extincetion Date: 2018



  • Baiji: Estimated Extinction Date: 2017



  • Western Black Rhinoceros: Estimated Extinction Date: 2006



  • Pyrenean Ibex: Estimated Extinction Date: 2000

If we can make our human lives better, why we ignore the lives of other species? When we are on our way making the world a better place, we didn't include the wildlife. When we leave something out, that means it's not the whole world yet, and we haven't made the wolrd a better place, just a part of our world is getting better

The smallest action in our daily live works, and it works even faster if we spread the words, we may think it's small but if we all do this together, we're doing a good thing for millions and billions time for the nature, and hopefully we're quick enough so it's not too late
